Earlier Life and Education

Prabhas was born into a Telugu-speaking family to film producer Uppalapati Surya Narayana Raju and Siva Kumari in Madras, Tamil Nadu. The youngest of three children, he has a elder brother, Prabodh, and elder sister, Pragathi.He is the nephew of Telugu film actor Krishnam Raju. His family hails from Mogalthur, near Bhimavaram of West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh.
Prabhas did his schooling at Don Bosco Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Chennai, and at DNR High School, Bhimavaram.[14] He then completed his intermediate education from Nalanda College, Hyderabad.He is also an alumnus of Satyanand Film Institute, Visakhapatnam.

PROJECT K:

Project K is a sci-fi film, it is a big-budget movie said to be made with Rs 500 crores, Prabhas will share the screen in this film along with Big B of Bollywood Amitabh Bachchan and Deepika Padukone for the first time.
Project K is a working title, it is yet to be titled by the makers, it will release in cinemas in two parts. As per the reports, the filming for the first part has been wrapped up, it is said to be in the post-production stage at present. While the production for the second part has already begun, the report states.
The tentative release date of the movie is April 2024, however, makers are yet to make an announcement regarding its release date.
